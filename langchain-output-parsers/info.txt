Output parser helps us to work with models who cant give structured output.


Output parse in Lnagchain helps us to convert the output of the
model into a structured format like JSON, Pydantic, TypedDict, etc.

instead of plain text. They ensure consistency, validation and ease of use in application.

4 major types of output parsers in langchain are:
1. JsonOutputParser
2. PydanticOutputParser
3. StringOutputParser
4. StructuredOutputParser
